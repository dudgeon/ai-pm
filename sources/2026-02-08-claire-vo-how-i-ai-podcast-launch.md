**Title:** BIG NEWS: Claire is the host of 'How I AI' on the Lenny's Podcast network

**Author:** Claire Vo
**Published:** April 23, 2025
**Read Time:** 3 minutes

Claire Vo announced the launch of her new podcast series called "How I AI," produced in partnership with the Lenny's Podcast network.

"Today I have some very special news," she begins, explaining that her experience exploring AI applications as a solo founder has inspired this venture. She notes, "every day there is more and more to learn" about AI capabilities.

The podcast features practical demonstrations with industry guests. "Each episode will be about 30 minutes, and our guests will walk you through a few specific workflows or use cases" while sharing their screens step-by-step.

Episodes are available on YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts, releasing every Monday. Vo expresses enthusiasm about the collaboration with Lenny's team, particularly their efforts supporting the product community's AI understanding.

She invites listener participation: "Have a great idea for a future guest? Please let us know" through an online form.

The post concludes with product updates, mentioning improvements to ChatPRD including chat functionality fixes, GPT-4.1 model upgrades, and Slack integration enhancements.
