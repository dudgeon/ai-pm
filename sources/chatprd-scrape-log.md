## Publication Overview

ChatPRD is Claire Vo's AI productivity platform. The **How I AI** podcast is the primary source — weekly ~30-minute episodes where guests demonstrate 2-3 AI workflows with screen-sharing. Claire Vo hosts all episodes.

The podcast launched April 2025 and is distributed through Lenny's Podcast network. Episodes are published on chatprd.ai/how-i-ai/ (with Lenny's as syndication partner).

ChatPRD also has a **blog** (chatprd.ai/blog) with written commentary, tool explainers, and workshop writeups.

## Skip Criteria

**Always scrape**: Guest demos of AI workflows (the show's core format) — all are relevant to ai-pm-craft regardless of guest background.

**Usually skip**:
- ChatPRD platform/product announcements
- Pure promotional content for ChatPRD features
- Non-substantive blog posts (e.g., recaps of events with no workflow content)
- Episodes where workflow is entirely domain-specific and non-transferable (rare for this show)
